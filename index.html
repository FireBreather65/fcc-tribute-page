Trixie Mattel

Drag queen, singer-songwriter, comedian, and enterpreneur

Trixie Mattel is the stage name of Brian Michael Firkus, an American drag queen, singer, songwriter, comedian, and entrepreneur. She rose to fame after competing on the seventh season and winning the third season of RuPaul's Drag Race All Stars. She is also known for her web series UNHhhh and I Like to Watch with Katya Zamolodchikova, her musical albums Two Birds, One Stone, Barbara, and The Blonde & Pink Albums, and her cosmetics company Trixie Cosmetics. She has also published a book, starred in a documentary, and appeared as a judge on Queen of the Universe.
